Updates
The Programmable Robotic Arm project aims to design, build, and program a versatile robotic arm capable of performing various manipulation tasks. This arm will be equipped with motors and sensors to ensure precise and responsive movements, and it will be controlled using a user-friendly interface.
The Line Following Robot project aims to design, build, and program a robot capable of following a line traced on the ground. The robot will use line sensors to detect contrast between the line and the surface, thereby adjusting its trajectory to remain on the predetermined path.
The Design and Assembly of a Multifunctional Drone project aims to create a versatile drone capable of performing various missions, such as surveillance, aerial mapping, photography, and other specialized applications. This drone will be designed considering flight stability, battery autonomy, video transmission quality, and remote control features.
